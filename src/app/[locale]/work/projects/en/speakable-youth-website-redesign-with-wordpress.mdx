## Overview

Speakable Youth is an Indonesian-based English community that serves as a dynamic meeting place for people from all over the world to learn, practice, and develop their English skills. Our focus is on providing a friendly and judgment-free platform, with Discord as the main hub, offering a variety of engaging resources and activities to improve your English skills.

## Key Features

- **Educational Resources**: Users can access a variety of learning materials, such as grammar guides, vocabulary lessons, and training articles. Each resource is designed to help participants deepen their English knowledge.
- **English Practice Sessions**: There are weekly interactive sessions where community members can practice speaking and listening, with topics covering culture, daily life, and current affairs.
- **Social Activities and Competitions**: Competitions such as English debates and vocabulary games add to the fun of learning, while developing skills in a natural and fun way.
- **Digital Library**: A comprehensive digital library of e-books, video tutorials, and practice exercises, providing instant access to additional learning materials to enrich the learning experience.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress**: The primary platform for the community website, allowing for easy content management, plugin integration, and responsive display across multiple devices.
- **Discord**: The primary community platform for real-time chat, voice calls, and screen sharing for members.
- **WordPress Plugins**: Leveraging plugins to support additional features, such as discussion forums, event calendars, and social media integration, helps increase member interactivity and engagement.

## Challenges and Learnings

Managing and maintaining an inclusive online community on platforms like Discord and WordPress is a huge challenge, especially in ensuring the comfort and engagement of every member. The process has provided many valuable lessons about the dynamics of online interactions and maintaining a positive and supportive community.

## Results

Speakable Youth is now an active and growing community, with positive contributions to its members in improving their English skills through interactive and fun activities. The WordPress website supports the user experience by providing up-to-date information and easy access to community activities.
